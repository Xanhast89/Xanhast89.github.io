Who We Are


Hello! Welcome to Little Pigeon!


My name is Hannah, I started sewing cotton rompers for my little one in 2023 and after encouragement from friends and family decided to embrace a life long dream and launch my own shop. My little cherub was given the pet name "Little Pigeon" by her Great Grandad, and what a perfectly adorable name for the dream brand.


My mum introduced me to my first sewing pattern when I could safely operate scissors by myself, and my nanna instilled the most meticulous sewing habits. I went on to study Fashion Design at Sheffield Hallam University. This has allowed me to create a romper fit for play, exploring and adventuring.


Each piece is handmade to order so please allow 14 days for your item to be sewn, wrapped and posted.
